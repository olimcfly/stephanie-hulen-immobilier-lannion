══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  /admin/modules/content/annuaire/index.php
  → dirname × 3 = /admin
  → require_once dirname(dirname(dirname(__DIR__))) . '/includes/diagnostic_tab.php';

  /admin/modules/system/settings/ai/index.php
  → dirname × 4 = /admin
  → require_once dirname(dirname(dirname(dirname(__DIR__)))) . '/includes/diagnostic_tab.php';

  Ou plus robuste (si BASE_PATH défini dans init.php) :
  → require_once BASE_PATH . '/admin/includes/diagnostic_tab.php';
